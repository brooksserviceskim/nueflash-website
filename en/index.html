03 — Services

discover.
shine. sell.

Choose one of three collaboration models based on your brand category, budget, and China market strategy. All services ship from a Korean Incheon warehouse, with cross-border delivery negotiated to Korean domestic-shipping rates.

SERVICE 01

campaign live

Campaign Live Selling · Main Service

One brand, seven broadcasts over one month. Our seven KOLs rotate across their own channels — wider reach and more stable revenue than any single mega-KOL broadcast. Solo Live (1 KOL) or Mini Campaign (2–4 KOLs) options also available.

  • GMV per broadcast: ₩5M ~ ₩20M (per KOL)
  • Commission from 35%
  • Flexible scale: Solo / 2–4 Mini / 7-KOL Full Campaign
  • Samples via Incheon warehouse → Hangzhou studio
  • Post-broadcast order aggregation → Incheon dispatch

SERVICE 02

mega kol

Mega-KOL Booking · Brand-Building

When China-market brand awareness is the priority, we can book solo broadcasts with mega-tier KOLs through Nueflash's network. Best for brands prioritizing brand-building over margin.

  • Beauty & Fashion categories only
  • Commission structure on request (varies by KOL)
  • Promotional vehicle — lower revenue efficiency than Campaign Live
  • Maximizes China-market brand exposure

SERVICE 03

paid post

Paid Content · Sponsorships

Commission paid promotional posts with our seven KOLs. Pure sponsorships (free styling at the KOL's discretion) are also available — posting cannot be enforced.

  • Paid promotions: separate consultation
  • Sponsorships: KOL's discretion, posting not enforced
  • Available on both Xiaohongshu & Douyin
  • Standalone or campaign package

04 — Campaign Process

one brand,
one month,
seven broadcasts.

Once a campaign is set — say, "*** Brand June Campaign" — our seven KOLs rotate over the month to live-sell the brand's products. Seven broadcasts total, each KOL curating based on their viewer persona. Beyond the full campaign (7 KOLs), Solo Live and 2–4 KOL Mini Campaigns are also possible.

STEP 01

Briefing & Contract

Align on brand category, SKUs, and China strategy. Finalize campaign schedule and commission.

STEP 02

KOL Pick & Sample Request

Our 7 KOLs personally pick from the lineup. Size-specific samples requested to Incheon warehouse.

STEP 03

Sample Dispatch

Incheon warehouse → Hangzhou studio direct shipping. Logistics billed directly by our warehouse partner.

STEP 04

Campaign Broadcasts

7 KOLs rotate over one month. 7 total live broadcasts across Douyin and Xiaohongshu.

STEP 05

Order Aggregation

Cumulative order data aggregated post-broadcast. Stock dispatched by brand to Incheon warehouse.

STEP 06

Fulfillment & Settlement

Direct shipping from Incheon to Chinese consumers. Commission settled per agreed cycle.
